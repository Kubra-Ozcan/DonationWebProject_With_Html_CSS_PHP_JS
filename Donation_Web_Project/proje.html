Can support verified real needs, -You can filter the needs you desire according to the space and type, -You can create a collective movement with like-minded people who want to support you with imece culture, You can be aware of the needs in your back street, in your neighborhood. Since 2015, more than 110 thousand members, more than 350 NGOs, more than 70 private sector organizations, hundreds of public institution representatives have used the Needs Map platform. While more than 9.88 million items of need were met through the platform, a sharing economy equivalent to approximately 490 million liras was created, creating a difference with a high social impact.
